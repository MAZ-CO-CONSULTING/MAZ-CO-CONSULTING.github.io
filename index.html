About Us

MAZ & CO CONSULTING is a multinational marketing consultancy founded in June 2018. Operating across the USA, South Africa, Zimbabwe, the UK, and Dubai, we help brands achieve greater visibility, engagement, and revenue growth worldwide.

We specialize in market research, strategy development, digital optimization, branding and campaign execution, blending creativity with data‑driven insights for sustainable results.
